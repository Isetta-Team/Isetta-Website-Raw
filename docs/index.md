# Welcome to Isetta Engine

We are a student project from Carnegie Mellon's Entertainment Technology Center.  
Our aim is to demystify the game engine development process.

## New here?
- We are making a game engine called Isetta to learn about game engine development. The Isetta engine is open-sourced [here](https://github.com/Isetta-Team/Isetta-Engine).
- We are documenting our whole process and recording all our thoughts, decisions and pitfalls in weekly blogs, starting from [Week 0](https://isetta.io/blogs/week-0/).
- We are interviewing with industry professionals for their informed input. The edited transcriptions are posted [here](https://isetta.io/interviews/AdamSerdar-interview/).

## Where are we now?
- We just released our latest build this week [here](https://github.com/Isetta-Team/Isetta-Engine/releases/tag/week-4).
- We just released our Week 4 blog post: [Buried in Comments](https://isetta.io/blogs/week-4/)
- We just interviewed with Casey Mutori! See what else Casey has to say about game engine programming in his full [interview](https://isetta.io/interviews/CaseyMuratori-interview/).
